Why Deposit?

For those with low risk appetite, fixed deposits provide perfect solution for safe and secure investment. Fixed deposit has traditionally been one of the first saving instruments in India because of the following advantages:

  • EASE OF BUYING: Easy for customer to buy fixed deposit as it is available both online and offline.
  • FIXED RETURNS: In the era of falling interest rates, Fixed deposits offer assured and stable returns.
  • ATTRACTIVE RETURNS FOR SENIOR CITIZENS: Extra rate of interest for senior citizens.
  • FLEXIBILITY OF TERM: Money can be invested in fixed deposits with a range from 1 year to 5 years.
  • FLEXIBILITY OF INVESTMENT: With amount ranging from Rs.10,000 to unlimited, it offers wide range of investment opportunity.
  • PENSION: Fixed deposit can be used as pension as interest is available at monthly /quarterly/half yearly basis.
  • AVENUE FOR SURPLUS FUNDS: Having the feature of guaranteed return, one would be assured while parking the funds for short/long term.
